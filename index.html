Who are the Monkees?

Listen to Tracks/ Watch Video/ Keep Track of New Releases/ Book the Band

Davy Jones, Mickey Dolenz, Michael Nesmith and Peter Tork are the Monkees. The band behind such hits as “Daydream Believer” and ‘I’m a Believer” welcome you to their site. TV stars and musicians and with 50 years of experience, the Monkees can guarantee a spectacular performance whether it’s your wedding or party. But wait, there’s more! Listen to our back catalogue, watch video and keep track of new releases right here on this site. We hope you enjoy your time and the site.
See you soon.
Davy, Mickey, Michael and Peter x
