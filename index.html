Davy Jones, Mickey Dolenz, Michael Nesmith and Peter Tork are the Monkees. The band behind such hits as “Daydream Believer” and ‘I’m a Believer” welcome you to their site. TV stars and musicians with 50 years of experience, the Monkees guarantee a spectacular performance whether it’s your wedding or party.


The Monkees consist of Davy Jones as lead vocalist for the band, Mickey Dolenz on drums, Michael Nesmith on lead guitar and finally Peter Tork on keyboard and piano. Starting out, the Monkees were formed by Bob Rafelson and Bert Schneider for the television show the Monkees, which aired from 1966 to 1966, before becoming a successful band in their own right.


The Monkees have sold more than 75 million records worldwide, making them one of the biggest selling groups of all time with such hits as "Last Train to Clarksville", "Stepping Stone", "Daydream Believer", and "I'm a Believer".


They’re also considered pioneers in the music industry; their televised perfomances on their show are considered precursors to the modern music video. And they happen to love the spotlight!


But wait, there’s more! Listen to our back catalogue, watch video and keep track of new releases right here on this site. We hope you enjoy your time here.
